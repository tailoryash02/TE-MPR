How to File Your Income Tax Return

Here are the steps to file your income tax return:

  1. Step 1: Gather all necessary financial documents.
  2. Step 2: Choose the right ITR form.
  3. Step 3: Fill out the ITR form with accurate information.
  4. Step 4: Calculate your tax liability and pay any due taxes.
  5. Step 5: Verify your ITR.
  6. Step 6: E-file your ITR on the official website.
  7. Step 7: Keep a copy of the ITR acknowledgment for your records.
